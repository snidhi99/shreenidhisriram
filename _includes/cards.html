Data Driven Evaluations of Student Groups

Ray Tracing

A parallelized ray tracing algorithm in CUDA based off of Kevin Beason's serialized implementation.

Project

Causal Topic Modeling

A python implementation of Iterative Topic Modeling with Time Series Feedback (ITMTF). We attempt to causally correlate textual data (i.e. news articles) with non-textual data (i.e. stock prices).

Technical Report

Adaboost Implementations on Distributed Systems: SparkBOOST vs Scikit-learn

A technical report seeking to determine the advantages of different implementations of the AdaBoostClassifier and at what point a distributed version of the algorithm outperforms its single-core counterpart.
